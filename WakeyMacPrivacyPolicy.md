# WakeyMac Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

WakeyMac is a lightweight macOS menu bar utility that helps keep your Mac awake. Your privacy is important, and WakeyMac is designed to work locally on your device.

## Information We Collect

WakeyMac does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.

The app does not require an account, does not use analytics, does not include advertising, and does not track users.

## Local App Settings

WakeyMac may store basic app preferences locally on your Mac, such as your selected language, default awake duration, display-awake preference, and sound effects setting.

These settings remain on your device and are not sent to the developer or any third party.

## Network Access

WakeyMac does not require internet access to provide its core functionality.

## Third-Party Services

WakeyMac does not use third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, tracking tools, or cloud services.

## System Permissions

WakeyMac uses macOS system APIs to prevent your Mac from sleeping while the app is active. This functionality runs locally on your device and does not involve collecting personal data.

## Children’s Privacy

WakeyMac does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
